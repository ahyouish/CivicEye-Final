Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How can we help you?

From the Citizen Dashboard, fill out the "Report a New Issue" form. You must attach a photo, write a short description, select an issue type (like 'Pothole' or 'Waste'), and pinpoint the location on the map before submitting.

You can report issues related to public infrastructure and services. Currently, we support categories for Potholes (handled by PWD), Streetlights (handled by the City Corporation), and Waste disposal issues.

Click on the "Track My Submitted Issues" button on your dashboard. This will take you to a page listing all the issues you've reported and their current status.

  • Pending: Your issue has been submitted and is waiting for review by the concerned department.
  • In Progress: Your issue has been acknowledged and work has begun to resolve it.
  • Completed: The issue has been successfully resolved.
  • Rejected: The issue was found to be invalid or a duplicate.

CIVIC EYE is a platform designed to empower citizens by providing a direct channel to report local civic issues to the correct government departments, ensuring transparency and efficient resolution.
